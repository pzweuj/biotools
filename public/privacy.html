English

We take your privacy seriously. Our concise policy is:

  • We do not collect any personal data from users of our website or apps.
  • We do not share any of your information with third parties.
  • Your inputs are stored only on your local device (e.g., browser local storage/cache) and are not uploaded to our servers.

Local Storage & Preferences

To provide a better experience, the site may save essential preferences on your device (e.g., interface and language settings). This data stays on your device, and you can clear it at any time via your browser.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change in the future, we will update this page accordingly.
